About Us

Authentic Swahili Dining Since 2010

Our Story

Bringing you the true taste of Zanzibar through traditional Swahili cuisine and locally sourced coffee

Nestled in the heart of Zanzibar, Asili Cafe has been celebrating the rich culinary heritage of the Swahili coast since 2010. Our two locations - one at Mtweni opposite Karume Institute and another at Kinazi Mmoja opposite Mmoja Hospital - serve as cultural bridges, connecting locals and visitors with authentic flavors that have been passed down through generations.

Learn More

Our Vision

To be Zanzibar's premier destination for authentic Swahili cuisine, preserving traditional flavors while creating memorable dining experiences. We envision Asili Cafe as a cultural culinary hub where every dish tells the story of our beautiful spice islands.

Traditional Swahili recipes passed down through generations
Fresh spices from Zanzibar's renowned spice farms
Seafood sourced daily from the Indian Ocean
